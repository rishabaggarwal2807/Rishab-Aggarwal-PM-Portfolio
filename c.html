About Me

I am a Product Manager with about 8 years of experience building products with only one motivation of impacting lives positively and meaningfully. I have a diverse experience of working across Invoicing & Taxation, Payments & Fintech, Media & OTT, Advertising and Mobility industries.

You can expect me to:

institute a balanced short term & long-term product vision
own and maintain a strategic backlog in sync with that vision
drive the backlog initiatives to success with end-to-end ownership
lead a product team and coach junior PMs
Vibe code and champion AI
and most importantly, manage all the stakeholders effectively with grace.

My Product Management Philosophy

• Try stepping into the shoes of your customer as often as possible
• Know your product like the back of your hand
• Never be afraid of digging deep and getting your hands dirty in research/data
• Hypothesis testing is a crucial step of the process
• Execution is everything- ship small and often
• Obsess over measurement: Metrics, KPIs, OKRs
• The most tech heavy solution may not be the best solution always
• Trust the product management process, and you shall see!
