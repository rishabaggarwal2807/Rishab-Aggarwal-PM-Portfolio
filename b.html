About Me

I am a Product Manager with 8 years of experience building products that positively shape user lives. My journey spans industries such as Invoicing & Taxation, Payments & Fintech, Media & OTT, Advertising, and Mobility, giving me a strong foundation in solving diverse, complex problems at scale.

What you can expect from me:

🎯 Crafting a balanced short- and long-term product vision
🗂️ Managing a strategic roadmap/backlog aligned with that vision
🚀 Driving initiatives from this roadmap with end-to-end ownership
🤝 Leading cross-functional product teams and mentoring junior PMs
🤖 Championing "meaningful" AI adoption and leveraging emerging tools
💬 Managing stakeholders with empathy and clarity

The kind of problems I have designed solutions for:

  • 🔑 Conversion Drops: Optimized subscription funnels and payment journeys for higher conversions.
  • 💔 User Churn: Reduced cancellations via churn insights & VoC programs.
  • 🧭 Complex Journeys: Simplified user flows, boosting upgrades and NPS.
  • 📊 Lack of Insights: Built analytics dashboards with embedded financial & user data.
  • 📩 Engagement Gaps: Launched real-time communication tools for user engagement.
  • ⚙️ Operational Inefficiencies: Automated monitoring, reducing downtime and support tickets.
  • 💰 Silos in Payments & Order Management: Designed integrated systems that unify fragmented payment and order workflows
